## Hero

> A transparent and responsive government is critical for the communities who depend on it.

At Clarity Value, we believe governments should have the best possible tools to improve people's lives. If your government seeks an integrated system for land management, case management, budgeting or data, we have what you need.

Our mission is simple: help government agencies work better for their constituents and employees.

## Mission

> Our mission is to help governments offer superior services to citizens and businesses.

### Values

**Affordability.** We believe public resources should be spent on public services – not overpriced technology. Our adaptable, cloud-based software allows us to charge governments less for a better solution.

**Accessibility.** We strive to make government more accessible, especially for the communities that rely most on public services. Our software is designed to be simple and intuitive for all users, whatever their age or education.

**Trust.** We follow through on our commitments to government customers. We also prioritize transparency and collaboration, whether we are exploring a potential partnership or configuring our software to suit a customer's unique needs.

**Relationships.** We seek to build partnerships with governments that will endure for years to come. Our solution is easy to update as government needs or processes change, and we provide hands-on customer support.
