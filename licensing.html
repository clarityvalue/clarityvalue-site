02  /  E-sign & issuance

A signature on a phone, a license in an inbox.

License terms presented in the portal. Applicant signs on a phone — same e-sign engine that runs employment contracts and vendor agreements. Counter-signed by the licensing authority. PDF certificate generated, emailed, and filed to the licensee record.

  • E-signNative, phone-friendly, audit-trailed. Same engine across HR, vendor, licensing.
  • CertificatePDF certificate generated with QR code that public can verify on your /license-lookup page.
  • WalletApple/Google Wallet pass for licenses inspectors check in the field.
03  /  Licensee CRM & renewals

Know your licensees the way a good neighbor knows them.

Every licensee has a record — every license they hold, every renewal, every inspection, every fee, every complaint, every email and SMS your office has ever sent them. Renewal notices go out automatically. Lapsed licenses surface for enforcement.

  • 360°One licensee, every interaction — same CRM thread that runs through 311, FOIA, and permits.
  • Renewals90/60/30-day notices by email and SMS. One-click renewal if nothing material changed.
  • EnforcementLapsed-license queue with action history. Hand off to inspections module when a site visit is needed.
