02  /  Ad-hoc reports

"Council asked for it Tuesday. Send it by Wednesday."

Filter, group, sort, chart. Save the configuration as a report. Schedule it to email itself. The clerk builds it without learning SQL — and the data engineer doesn't get pulled in.

  • FilterCross-module filtering — permits issued by district, district by quarter.
